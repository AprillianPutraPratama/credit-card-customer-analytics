# Credit Card Customer Analytics & Risk Profile Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project delivers a comprehensive end-to-end data analysis on credit card customer behavior, revenue streams, demographic segmentation, and credit risk profiles. The interactive dashboard was built using **Google Looker Studio** as part of the **MySkill Data Analysis Specialization**.

## 🔍 Key Insights & Business Findings

### 1. Customer Demographics & Behavior
* **Marital Status:** Over 51.2% of total credit card holders are married.
* **Top Spending Categories:** Primary spending is driven by **Bills**, **Fuel**, and **Entertainment**.
* **Occupation:** *Self-employed* and *Businessman* represent the largest customer base.

### 2. Revenue & Profitability Analysis
* **High-Value Segments:** *Businessman* and *White-collar* professionals generate the highest transaction volumes and interest earned for the bank.
* **Credit Utilization:** *Retirees* and *Government employees* display the highest credit utilization ratios (~28-29%), indicating strong reliance on credit lines.

### 3. Credit Risk & Delinquency Profiling
* Risk analysis tracks delinquent accounts across US states and customer education levels to help optimize risk management and credit line assignment.

---

## 💡 Strategic Business Recommendations
1. **Targeted Campaigns:** Develop tailored credit card rewards focusing on **Bills & Fuel Cashback** for the high-spending *Businessman* and *White-collar* segments.
2. **Risk Mitigation:** Closely monitor credit line limits for high-utilization groups (*Retirees* & *Govt*) to maintain low delinquency rates.
